## Benchmark Themes
- Best-in-class products make empty states educational, not dead.
- The best command/search experiences are deterministic before they are magical.
- Trust comes from honest scope, visible access boundaries, and fewer misleading controls.
- Civis should win by combining breadth with coherence and operational honesty.
