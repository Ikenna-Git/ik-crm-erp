## Where Civis Wins
- Stronger founder / workspace admin governance story than most SMB CRM tools.
- Better honest setup posture than products that hide incomplete provider states.
- Command-centre framing across CRM, approvals, people, and finance is differentiated.

## Where Civis Still Trails
- Live validation evidence is still thinner than mature competitors.
- Marketing remains preview-only.
- Broader responsive and empty-state polish is still uneven across some modules.

## Competitive Direction
- Keep the calm premium aesthetic.
- Win on clarity, governance, and AI-guided next actions.
- Avoid competing on fake breadth. Compete on connected workflows and honest control.
