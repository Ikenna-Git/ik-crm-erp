# Premium Positioning Review

Date: 2026-06-07
Product: Civis CRM & ERP

## One-line Positioning
Civis is the AI-native CRM and ERP command centre for teams that need sales, finance, people, operations, approvals, and governance in one workspace.

## Hero Options
1. The AI-native CRM and ERP command centre for governed teams.
2. Run CRM, finance, people, approvals, and operations from one calm workspace.
3. One governed operating layer for growing companies that need clarity before complexity.

## Tagline Options
- AI-guided. Role-aware. Approval-ready.
- One governed workspace for sales, finance, people, and operations.
- Calm control for teams that have outgrown disconnected tools.

## Product Promise
Civis helps a growing company see what matters, route work safely, and guide each team through the next action without losing control boundaries.

## What Civis Is
- An AI-guided CRM and ERP operating layer.
- A governed workspace for CRM, Accounting, HR, Operations, Inventory, Projects, Portal, Docs, and Admin.
- A role-aware product with founder/platform boundaries and org-scoped admin surfaces.

## What Civis Is Not
- Not just a dashboard template.
- Not a fake AI wrapper that claims actions happened when they did not.
- Not a public self-serve billing product yet.
- Not a proven compliance platform until evidence exists.

## Top 5 Differentiators
1. Civis Guide: deterministic navigation and product guidance even when provider keys are missing.
2. Founder/platform boundary model: founder-only controls stay separate from workspace admin power.
3. Privacy locks: HR and Accounting stay session-locked even for authorized managers until intentionally unlocked.
4. Honest setup posture: provider-backed features fail clearly instead of faking success.
5. Command-centre framing: CRM, approvals, finance, people, and ops are presented as one operating layer, not isolated modules.

## Buyer Personas
- Founder or CEO who needs visibility without losing control.
- Operations lead who needs approvals, workflows, and clear ownership.
- Finance lead who needs invoices, expenses, exports, and governance.
- HR or People Ops manager who needs privacy-respecting employee workflows.
- Growing admin team replacing disconnected tools and manual follow-through.

## Launch-safe Messaging
- AI-guided workspace
- governed workspace
- role-aware admin controls
- approval-ready
- privacy locked
- preview-only where required
- requires setup where provider validation is missing
- evidence pending for trust operations still being validated

## Claims To Avoid
- enterprise-grade compliance
- certified security posture
- validated backup / restore unless evidence is recorded
- live self-serve billing
- fully autonomous AI agents
- fake customer logos, metrics, testimonials, or market share claims
